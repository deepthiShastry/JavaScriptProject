TRAIN

The easiest way to get there from elsewhere in Belgium or even abroad from Leuven is by train. There are direct and indirect connections from most Belgian cities, as well as from Brussels National Airport. For travel times, look best on the site of the SNCB , the National Society of Belgian Railways.


BUS

If you are in the vicinity of the city or in a village without a train station, the bus is also an option. For bus journeys and routes in Flanders, visit the website of De Lijn , the Flemish bus company.


CAR
